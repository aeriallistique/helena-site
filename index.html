About Totem

Totem Gymnastics Club based in central Manchester provides recreational gymnastics sessions, catering for ages 2-16 years. Weekly sessions are split into year groups, 4-6 years and 7+ years. For our schedule please see the link below where you can book your free trial session for your child.
Totem also offers private tuition outside of the usual sessions which are available via request.We also offer aerial silks classes to children aged 8+. This class is perfect for beginners who are just starting aerial. Teaching the foundations of aerial arts and having fun is the essence of the class. This class is available to book via the booking site.

What to expect at a Totem class

COVID-19

Due to the current situation with Covid-19, it would be extremely helpful if you would come with your child wearing their gymnastics leotard/active wearing underneath their clothes to ease with dropping off your child for the lesson. When you arrive, all adult visitors will be temperature tested and everyone will be asked to wash their hands. You will then be directed up the stairs where the class will take place. Durin these times, parents/carers will be asked to stay in their car or leave and return where possible. Masks are optional when entering the building but whilst the children are partaking in the session, no masks are advised to be worn. Sosical distancind will be ensured by creating stations that are used by one child at a time and adequately spaced out - where a cross over of equipment is needed, the equipment will be cleaned beforehand. As with all equipent prior to use will be clean before and after all sessions.

So what will my child be learning at Totem Gymnastics?

No matter what age of the child, creating a safe, fun and friendly environment is the priority of the club. Sessions start with a fun and engaging warm-up inluding pulse raisers, stretches and conditioning exercises. Children will learn the fundamentals of gymnastics and improve flexibility and strength through a diverse and fun approach. All the sessions are different each week and we work on 4 areas of gymnastics, floor work, beam work, vault and bars. All sessions finish with a cool down and a big well done!

How do you track your child's progress?

Here at Totem, we believe it's so important for every gymnast to be able to track their progress as well as parents/carers to see your child's progress. Gymasts are assessed in the BAGA proficiency awards every 3 months, which is a very in formal Assessment. This is the gymnasts opportunitty to showcase thei skills they have been learning in the sessions. To gain their badges, they have to 7 out of 10 skills on each badge to pass. This is a great encouragement for improving their skills and an amazing achievement for the gymnasts.

FAQ

What should my child wear to classes?

For gymnastics classes, light and comfortable clothing is recommend. For aerial, leggings and a top that can be tucked in is recommended. Leotards under leggings is also suitable. No crop tops.

Can I stay and watch my child’s class?

Due to the spacing we can’t offer the option to watch your child’s class.

Do I need to stay with my child during the session?

No, you don’t have to stay and currently whilst we are social distancing we ask all parents to stay in their car or leave and return for your child whilst the session is on.

Is there a minimum commitment for classes?

All the classes are on a rolling subscription. Please get into contact with Helena regarding cancellation.

How do I pay for my child’s classes?

All classes are paid via classforkids. Payment is taken by card. Please note: all classes must be pre-booked via the booking site to attend.

What should I do if we can’t attend one week?

No need to do anything as the subscriptions run on a monthly basis and the cost is for the month inclusive.
